Thematic Concentration

Details

Statistical methods are crucial in many fields, and are applied in virtually all areas of human inquiry. Any education in statistics should include exposure to the use of statistical ideas and applications in subject-area contexts. Therefore, students in the B.Sc. Major in Statistics program are required to fulfill a nine-credit "thematic concentration". The requirement is that a student take 9 upper-level (300 or above) credits in a theme area, with at least most of these courses having clear statistical or probabilistic content.
